About Me

Greetings, my name is Tou Feng Lo and currently a part-time student at St. Paul College. My major as of now is Computer Graphics and Visualization. I’m hoping to graduate in the Fall of 2023. I work as a PCA at Home Express HealthCare. During my free time, I really enjoy doing photography. As of right now, I consider myself a freelance photographer. I was inspired to do photography when I came across some awesome pictures online. Their work showed me that a person can either think inside the box or outside the box. In other words, a picture can be simple and beautiful with minor touch up editing, or a crazy abstract editing that will make a picture uniquely different. With that said, I was encouraged to go and buy myself a digital camera for photography. The pictures you see on this website will reflect my journey as a photographer. I want to use the skills that I’d learn in college to help elevate my career and the work that'll come in the future.
